OUR FEATURES

Effective communication and Seamless collaboration

The e-prescription provided by the doctor will be immediately accessible to the partner pharmacy enabling seamless communication and collaboration amongst the doctors, patients and the partner pharmacies thus making the whole process easier and quicker.

Intelligent follow- ups
and reminders

Mental health professionals can schedule appointments on future dates and can also book time slots during which they would like the patients to consult them. The appointments can be cancelled and rescheduled as per the convenience and availability of the mental health professional.

A comprehensive web-based platform

Integrated medical web based application suite for mental health professionals like neurologists, psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, counselors and other allied mental health professionals.

Scientific Diagnosis & Digital Prescription

Designed to aid MHPs in accurately diagnosing the mental illness which a patient is suffering from, basis the symptoms which the patient exhibits. using ICD 10 classification of mental and behavioural disorders.

MENTDOC IS SOLELY MEANT FOR MHPS

MentDoc has been designed exclusively for the mental health domain. It is meant for Mental health professionals like psychiatrists, psychologists, psychoanalysts, psychotherapists & mental health counselors.

Electronic Health Record (EHR)

An Electronic Health Record(EHR)is a digital version of a patient’s conventionally used paper-based summaries and records. This feature facilitates quick and secure access to the patient’s information for authorized mental health professio.
