ABOUT MentDoc, MentCom and Enrich

The Mental Healthcare Act 2017 has brought reformative changes to enhance the availability and scope of Mental Health services in the Country. In view of the fact that a large part of the population i.e. nearly 10-20 million Indians are currently suffering from complex mental disorders, such as schizophrenia and bipolar disorder and nearly 50 million Indians are suffering from mental illness like depression and anxiety, MentDoc was conceptualised with the goal to support the mandate of the Mental Healthcare Act 2017 for provisions of maintenance of records and reporting by Mental Health Professionals, and the right of the patient to assess basic medical records “a person with mental illness is entitled to receive documented medical information, pertaining to his diagnosis, investigation, assessment, and treatment as per the medical record”. MentDoc was initiated as India’s 1stElectronic Mental Health Record Solution, as well Practice Management & Patient Engagement Solution to assist mental health professionals in diagnosis, planning investigations, treatment, recording, maintaining reports and documenting Therapy, scheduling appointments, providing e-prescriptions, manage consultations, etc.

MentCom is an application designed to connect patients with Mental Health Professionals. With MentCom the patient can schedule appointments, view their diagnosis history, access their prescription, lab and test results, and upload symptom log/s.

Enrich is an online platform which has been designed with a practical and solutions-focused approach, driven towards skill-development. Enrich aims to enhance every adolescent by helping them develop effective social, emotional, academic and career skills ranging from self-awareness and self-reflection to 21-st century skills of problem-solving and critical thinking.

The programs at Enrich are divided into 3 distinct parts: Mirror, Guidance and Enrichment, for each domain – Social and Emotional, Academic, and Career.

  • Mirror: Assessment and Screening Tools
  • Guidance: Online Counseling Programs
  • Enrichment: Programs for Self-Enrichment
